Our Mission

Built on the Frontier.
For the Frontier.

Null System Workshop was founded on a simple principle: the colonists, independent operators, and frontier groups pushing the boundaries of explored space deserve the same quality of engineering as anyone operating in core systems — if not better.

For over 15 years, we've built, retrofitted, and repaired thousands of vessels from our base at Outer Rim Station 7, extending our reach across 12 sectors of frontier space. We don't just build ships — we build the infrastructure that makes the Fringes livable.

Our mission is to deliver frontier-grade engineering without compromise: no hidden costs, no inflated timelines, no tech that can't be supported at the edge of explored space.

Our History

From One Dock to Twelve Sectors

What started as a single repair bay on Outer Rim Station 7 has grown into one of the most trusted independent shipbuilders operating in the Fringes. Here's how we got here.

2009
Founded at Outer Rim Station 7

Null System Workshop opens its first repair bay with a crew of 12 engineers. Initial focus: emergency field repairs and basic hull maintenance for independent operators passing through the station.

2013
First Full Hull Fabrication Contract

Awarded our first full custom hull contract for the Kepler Reach Colonial Authority — a 6-vessel fleet for their expanding settlement support program. Delivered on time and under budget.

2017
Expansion to 5 Sectors

NSW deploys its first mobile repair barge fleet, extending service coverage across 5 frontier sectors. Emergency response capability established for the first time beyond Station 7.

2021
Launch of the NSW Product Lineup

Introduction of the NSW standardized vessel product line — 11 purpose-built designs covering military, industrial, and civilian classifications. First commercial catalog in the Fringe region.

2026
12-Sector Coverage & 250+ Deliveries

Today, NSW operates across 12 frontier sectors with a permanent staff of 400+ engineers, field technicians, and logistics specialists. 250 vessels delivered with a 98% mission success record.
