What We Offer

Our Services

From hull fabrication to field repairs, we provide the full spectrum of shipbuilding and maintenance services to keep your fleet operational across every frontier sector.

01
⚙️
Hull Fabrication

Full-scale construction of custom spacecraft hulls from initial design brief to launch-ready delivery. Built to withstand the harshest conditions in deep-space operations, from radiation corridors to high-pressure environments.

Custom hull geometry and structural engineering
Material selection for mission-specific environments
ORAC compliance certification included
Delivery timelines from 21–90 days
02
🔧
Refit & Retrofit

Comprehensive upgrade packages for existing vessels — from propulsion overhauls and weapons system integration to full interior reconfiguration. We breathe new operational life into aging hulls with modern systems and structural reinforcement.

Drive system replacement and calibration
Third-party module integration
Structural reinforcement and hull plating
Interior reconfiguration and life support upgrades
03
🛰️
Emergency Field Repairs

Mobile repair barges deployable across frontier sectors within 24–72 hours of a distress signal. Our field engineering teams are equipped to handle critical damage scenarios, from breach sealing to drive restoration, in hostile environments.

24/7 emergency response activation
Multi-sector coverage with pre-positioned assets
Hull breach sealing and atmospheric restoration
On-site diagnostics and component replacement
04
📐
Custom Design

End-to-end bespoke vessel design for unique operational requirements. Our engineering team works directly with clients from concept through prototype to deliver vessels that no off-the-shelf platform can match.

Mission requirement analysis and brief development
3D structural modeling and simulation
Iterative prototype review process
Full IP ownership transfer on delivery
05
🔗
Systems Integration

Expert installation, wiring, and calibration of complex third-party systems — weapons arrays, navigation suites, communications infrastructure, and specialized industrial equipment — into existing or new hull configurations.

Cross-platform compatibility assessment
Weapons and defense system installation
Navigation and sensor suite integration
Post-install diagnostic certification
06
📦
Logistics Support

Dedicated fleet and cargo logistics for corporations operating across multiple sectors. We provide consistent, reliable inter-sector shipping solutions backed by dedicated support crews and route management infrastructure.

Fleet scheduling and route optimization
Cargo handling and secure transfer
Dedicated support crew assignment
Multi-sector coordination and tracking
